Instructions

Welcome to the Pig Game! The object of the game is to reach a score of 20 or above before the other player. To start, Player 1 will start "rolling" the die by clicking on the roll dice button. If the die lands on a number from 2 to 6, you can either press hold to deposit that value from your current score to your overall score and it will then be Player 2's turn, OR you can keep playing by pressing roll. If you roll a 1, Player 2's turn begins and you lose all the points that you currently have. First one to 20 wins. Good luck!
